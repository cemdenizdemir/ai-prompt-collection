# THE FORCED FEYNMAN METHOD

PROMPT:

“I just studied [TOPIC].

I am going to explain what I understood as if you were a 10-year-old child.

While I explain, stop me every time I use jargon without knowing what it means, skip a step in the reasoning, or simplify so much that it becomes wrong.

At the end, tell me exactly what these mistakes reveal about what is still not solid in my mind.”
