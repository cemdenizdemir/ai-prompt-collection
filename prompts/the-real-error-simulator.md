# THE REAL ERROR SIMULATOR

PROMPT:

“Do not explain [CONCEPT] to me.

Put me directly into a real situation where I would have to use it and would probably make a mistake.

When I make a mistake, do not give me the answer. Ask me a question that forces me to discover where my reasoning is broken.

Only give me the answer after I have tried at least two times.

Repeat this cycle until I can get it right without hesitation.”
